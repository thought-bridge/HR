About ThoughtBridge

Built on 27 years of knowing what "the right fit" actually looks like.

ThoughtBridge HR Solutions was founded in 2013 as an executive search firm for mid-to-senior mandates. What began as a focused search desk is now a team that understands the geography, culture and hiring pace of Africa, the Middle East and Asia Pacific as well as it understands India.

The founder

Kashmira leads from the front, not the org chart.

With over 27 years of experience, Kashmira has led large recruitment teams and managed marquee clients across Indian and overseas markets, with a particular depth in Africa and the Middle East. Her team knows the modus operandi for head-hunting talent that fits African markets, and is equally equipped for positions looking toward India and Asia Pacific.
